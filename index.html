About

Fifty8Sports is a full-service sports management agency that was established in 2022. With offices in Istanbul, Doha, Nairobi, and Dar es Salam, we have a strong presence in some of the key sports markets in the world. Our agency has two departments: Talent Management & Representation and Sports Agency, both of which are dedicated to providing top-notch services to our clients.
